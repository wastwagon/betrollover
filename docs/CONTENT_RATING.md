# Content Rating for App Store & Play Console

BetRollover is a betting tips platform with real-money features. Set the appropriate content rating before submission.

## Apple App Store Connect (17+)

1. Go to [App Store Connect](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com) → Your App → App Information.
2. Under **Age Rating**, click **Edit**.
3. Complete the questionnaire. For BetRollover:
   - **Gambling and Contests**: Yes – the app facilitates or encourages gambling.
   - **Unrestricted Web Access**: No (unless you have in-app browser with unrestricted access).
   - **Simulated Gambling**: No – real money is involved.
4. Apple will typically assign **17+** for gambling apps.
5. Ensure your app metadata (description, keywords) does not target users under 17.

## Google Play Console (18+)

1. Go to [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console) → Your App → Policy → App content.
2. Open **Target audience and content** → **Target audience**.
3. Set **Target age groups** to include **18 and over**.
4. Open **Target audience and content** → **News apps** (or **Finance** if applicable).
5. Complete the **Content rating** questionnaire:
   - Go to **Content rating** → **Start questionnaire**.
   - Select **Gambling** as a content type.
   - Answer that the app involves real-money gambling.
6. Google will typically assign **18+** (PEGI 18 / Adults only).
7. In **Store listing** → **Content rating**, ensure the rating is displayed correctly.

## Summary

| Store        | Recommended Rating | Notes                                      |
|--------------|--------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| App Store    | 17+                | Gambling apps require 17+ minimum          |
| Play Console | 18+                | Gambling apps require 18+ in most regions   |
